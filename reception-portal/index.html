What you'll learn

The reception curriculum

Four certified modules covering everything a Legal Affairs receptionist needs — from the first greeting to handling confidential matters with discretion.

MODULE 01
Welcoming Visitors
First impressions, the official greeting, bilingual courtesy, and representing the Department with poise and warmth.
MODULE 02
Telephone & Enquiries
Professional call handling, transferring and triaging enquiries, and managing the switchboard during busy periods.
MODULE 03
Confidentiality & Security
Handling sensitive legal matters, visitor verification, data-protection duties, and escalation procedures.
MODULE 04
Scheduling & Systems
Appointment booking, meeting-room coordination, the visitor log, and the digital tools used at the front desk.

The shift

Dubai redefined the front desk

Across the Government of Dubai, "customer service centres" became Customer Happiness Centres, and "service employees" became Happiness Employees. The job changed with the name — from processing a queue to owning the visitor's outcome.

The old desk
A receptionist
Greets, logs, and points to a counter
Hands the visitor off and moves on
Measured by speed at the desk
The new standard
A Service Ambassador
Welcomes, understands, and resolves or routes
Connects people to the right service or channel
Measured by the visitor's happiness
The Customer Happiness Formula

What "happiness" means in practice

The UAE's national model sets out a simple formula for great government service — three ingredients, and the behaviours every ambassador brings to the desk.

1
Proud people
Employees who take genuine pride in delivering an excellent government service.
2
Committed entity
A department dedicated, end-to-end, to making every customer happy.
3
Positive customers
Visitors who leave informed, respected and confident — and stay positive.
Welcoming Courteous Professional Understanding Positive Innovative
What you'll be able to do

Not just greet — enable

A Service Ambassador turns "please take a seat" into "here's exactly how we'll sort this." That means knowing Dubai's services and channels well enough to guide people straight to them.

Connect to digital services
Guide visitors into DubaiNow — one app, 250+ services from 35+ entities — and help them sign in with UAE PASS instead of queuing.
Bundle the whole journey
Think like Dubai's one-stop shop: resolve a visitor's need as a complete bundle, so they don't get bounced between departments.
Inclusive, accessible service
Serve every visitor with confidence — including autism & sensory awareness and multilingual support, in the spirit of Dubai Way.
Discretion & trust
Handle sensitive legal matters with the confidentiality and security a Legal Affairs front desk demands.
The standard you'll be held to

Dubai measures happiness

Government entities are scored every year on customer happiness, employee happiness, and a live Mystery Shopper survey. This is the bar an ambassador is trained to meet — and yes, you may be mystery-shopped.

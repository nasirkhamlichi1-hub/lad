A few things worth saying

Real facts from the Department's services — the kind that put a visitor at ease. Each one is true, and each one points to a service you already know.

0
Free legal advice — genuinely free
Through Voluntary Legal Services, 395 volunteer lawyers help members of the public at no cost — a visitor picks an area of law and a firm, and can talk in their preferred language.
Pro Bono service
0
Half price for new advocates
A newly registered advocate pays 50% off the registration fee for their first three years on the Roll — a real encouragement for lawyers starting out in Dubai.
Registration of Advocates

The desk that never closes
Most of the Department's services are fully digital and available around the clock — on the Smart App, the website, and DubaiNow. Often your best help is pointing to the right channel.
Service channels
1 day
Some things are same-day
A replacement registration card, a name or office amendment, a 'to whom it may concern' certificate — each is turned around in a single working day. Reassuring news for an anxious visitor.
Registration & certificates
Instant
Check any lawyer is registered
The Advocates & Legal Consultants Directory lets anyone confirm a lawyer or firm is licensed in Dubai — free, instantly, just by searching a name. A simple way to protect a visitor.
Legal Directory
AED 0
Some complaints settle by conversation
Under Law No. 16 of 2025, a claim against a government entity is resolved by amicable settlement — no fees and no court, with a response due within set timelines. Calm, fair, and free.

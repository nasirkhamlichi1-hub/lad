## SECTION 4 — The Department's 11 services

**Lesson 4.1 — Eleven services to know by heart**
- **Statement:** `11 main services. 45 sub‑services. One confident ambassador.`
- **Accordion block** — one row per service (title = service; body = "For / Channel / What it is"):
  1. **Registration of Advocates & Legal Consultants** — *For:* Advocates & legal consultants · *Channel:* Legal Professions System (LPS) / Smart App. `Join and maintain a place on the Roll of practising advocates and legal consultants. New advocate registration AED 2,020, ~14 working days; new advocates get 50% off the fee for their first 3 years.`
  2. **Licensing of Advocacy & Legal Consultancy Firms** — *For:* Law & consultancy firms · *Channel:* LPS / Invest in Dubai. `Licence and manage a firm — partners, managers, name, address, activities, and cessation. New licence AED 3,020, ~20 working days.`
  3. **Violations & Fines** — *For:* Advocates & legal consultants · *Channel:* LPS / Smart App. `Settle professional fines, arrange instalments, or grieve a fine. Settlement & instalments: no fee.`
  4. **Voluntary Legal Services (Pro Bono)** — *For:* Members of the public · *Channel:* Voluntary Legal Services Smart Portal / Smart App / DubaiNow. `Free legal advice — the public pick an area of law and a firm. 395 lawyers across 48 areas of law. Free, instant.`
  5. **Professional Training & Development (CLPD)** — *For:* Advocates, consultants, training providers · *Channel:* CLPD Portal. `Register in CLPD activities (AED 1,050); providers gain accreditation and accredit courses (no fee, ~30 days).`
  6. **Representation in Government Cases** — *For:* Dubai Government entities · *Channel:* Central Legal Services Portal (Gov Portal). `The Department represents the Government before the courts and in arbitration. No fee.`
  7. **Execution of Judgments & Instruments** — *For:* Government entities & individuals · *Channel:* Gov Portal / Email. `Execute judgments and instruments — cheques, administrative resolutions, criminal judgments. No fee.`
  8. **Government Legal Support** — *For:* Dubai Government entities · *Channel:* Gov Portal. `Legislation, legal advice, company governance, IP, and contracts. No LAD fee.`
  9. **Government & Legal Professional Complaints** — *For:* Public & private organisations · *Channel:* Website / Smart App. `Claim against a government entity (Law No. 16 of 2025 — no fee, amicable settlement), or complain about an advocate/consultant (Professional Conduct).`
  10. **Monitoring & Inspection** — *For:* The public & the profession · *Channel:* Report / field inspection. `Report unlicensed legal practice and breaches of professional conduct. No fee.`
  11. **Informational Services — Legal Directory** — *For:* Everyone · *Channel:* Website / Smart App. `Verify whether an advocate, consultant or firm is registered/licensed. Search by name. Free, instant.`

## SECTION 7 — A visitor says… what do you do? (scenarios)

**Lesson 7.1 — Real moments at the desk**
Build each scenario as a **Flashcard** (front = the visitor's words; back = what to do)
**or** a **Scenario block** (situation → reveal the model response). Seven cards:

1. **Front:** `"I just graduated in law — how do I become a lawyer here?"`
   **Back:** `Welcome them warmly. This is New Registration. They apply on the Legal Professions System or Smart App — AED 2,020, ~14 working days, with 50% off the fee for their first 3 years. Offer the document list: degree, passport, good‑conduct certificate, Ministry of Justice training certificate, MOHRE‑attested contract and CV.`
2. **Front:** `"A lawyer treated me badly and took my money!"`
   **Back:** `Stay calm and empathetic. This is a complaint against an advocate/legal consultant: file via the website or Smart App; it goes to the Professional Conduct process (Professional.conduct@legal.dubai.gov.ae). Don't take sides — note the lawyer's name and the details.`
3. **Front:** `"I can't afford a lawyer but I really need legal advice."`
   **Back:** `Reassure them — the Department offers free Voluntary Legal Services (Pro Bono). Help them register on the Voluntary Legal Services Smart Portal (also Smart App / DubaiNow), choose the area of law, and they'll be matched with a volunteer lawyer. No fee.`
4. **Front:** `"How do I know if 'XYZ Law Firm' is actually licensed?"`
   **Back:** `Great question to encourage. Use the Advocates & Legal Consultants Directory on the website — search by the firm or person's name (full or partial) to confirm they're licensed. Offer to show them how.`
5. **Front:** `"Our government department needs a contract reviewed — urgently."`
   **Back:** `That's Government Legal Support via the Central Legal Services Portal (Gov Portal) — contract drafting, review and advice. Typically 10–30 working days by request type; no fee. Help them find the portal and the documents to attach.`
6. **Front:** `"I want to file a criminal case against my neighbour!"`
   **Back:** `Empathise, then redirect correctly. The Legal Affairs Department does NOT handle criminal reports or complaints — politely direct them to Dubai Police / Public Prosecution. (For a non‑criminal dispute with a government entity, the Department can help under Law No. 16 of 2025.)`
7. **Front:** `(A visitor using a wheelchair approaches your desk.)`
   **Back:** `Lead with the Charter: serve People of Determination in line with their needs. Proactively offer help, make sure the channel is accessible, be patient, and guide them through the service or smart channel yourself.`

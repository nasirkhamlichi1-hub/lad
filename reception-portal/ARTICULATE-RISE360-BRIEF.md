### 4.2 Registration of Advocates & Legal Consultants
- Intro: `Join and maintain a place on the Roll of practising advocates and legal consultants — the gateway to practising law in Dubai.`
- **Table:**
  | Sub‑service | Fee | Time |
  |---|---|---|
  | New advocate registration | AED 2,020 | 14 working days |
  | New legal‑consultant registration | AED 2,020 | 18 working days |
  | Registration renewal | from AED 1,020 | Immediate |
  | Reinstatement of registration | AED 2,020 | 10–14 working days |
  | Amend name/nationality, change office, or transfer roll | AED 520 | 1 working day |
  | Right of audience — Court of Cassation | AED 520 | 1 working day |
  | Replacement registration card | AED 120 | 1 working day |
  | 'To whom it may concern' certificate | AED 220 | 1 working day |
  | Temporary rights of audience permit | AED 5,020 | 5 working days |
  | Visiting legal‑consultant permit | AED 220 | 2 working days |
- **Note:** `Fees include a AED 10 innovation fee + AED 10 knowledge fee. New advocates get 50% off the registration fee for their first 3 years.`
- **Required documents (checklist):** `University law degree` · `Passport` · `Good‑conduct (criminal status) certificate` · `Certificate of passing the Ministry of Justice theoretical & practical training` · `Employment contract attested by MOHRE` · `Certificate from the commercial‑licensing authority confirming no commercial licences in the applicant's name (not required if Dubai‑issued)` · `Curriculum vitae (CV)`
- **Process — The journey:** `Apply` (LPS or Smart App) → `Review` (Department reviews the documents) → `Pay` (AED 2,020) → `Card issued` (decision + registration card; ~14 working days).

### 4.3 Licensing of Advocacy & Legal Consultancy Firms
- Intro: `Licence and manage an advocacy or legal‑consultancy firm in Dubai — partners, managers, name, address, activities, and cessation.`
- **Table:**
  | Sub‑service | Fee | Time |
  |---|---|---|
  | New firm licence (sole establishment, company or branch) | AED 3,020 (AED 3,000 per partner for companies) | 20 working days |
  | Firm licence renewal | AED 3,000 per advocate / consultant | 1 working day |
  | Amend licence — name, address, manager, activity, partner, legal form, or add a branch | AED 1,020 | 5 working days |
  | Temporary or permanent cessation | No fee | 5 working days |
  | 'To whom it may concern' certificate | AED 220 | 1 working day |
- **Note:** `Fees include a AED 10 innovation fee + AED 10 knowledge fee. New advocates get 50% off the licence fees for their first 3 years.`
- **Required documents (checklist):** `Detailed experience certificate from the previous employer (for advocates with under 2 years' practice)` · `Owners of a legal office outside Dubai: an undertaking naming a proposed UAE‑national managing advocate` · `Signed acceptance from the proposed manager to join the Dubai office full‑time` · `Proof the proposed manager is registered with the Department or the Ministry of Justice (or meets the Article 7 requirements)` · `Proof the proposed manager has at least 2 consecutive years' legal experience`
- **Process:** `Apply` (LPS / Invest in Dubai) → `Review` → `Pay` (AED 3,020) → `Licence issued` (no‑objection certificate + professional licence; ~20 working days).

## SECTION 7 — A visitor says… what do you do? (scenarios)
**Lesson 7.1 — Real moments at the desk.** Build each as a **Scenario block** (situation
→ reveal the model response) or a **Flashcard** (front = visitor's words; back =
response). Seven cards:
1. **`"I just graduated in law — how do I become a lawyer here?"`** → `Welcome them warmly. This is New Registration. They apply on the Legal Professions System or Smart App — AED 2,020, ~14 working days, with 50% off the fee for their first 3 years. Offer the document list: degree, passport, good‑conduct certificate, Ministry of Justice training certificate, MOHRE‑attested contract and CV.`
2. **`"A lawyer treated me badly and took my money!"`** → `Stay calm and empathetic. This is a complaint against an advocate/legal consultant: file via the website or Smart App; it goes to the Professional Conduct process (Professional.conduct@legal.dubai.gov.ae). Don't take sides — note the lawyer's name and the details.`
3. **`"I can't afford a lawyer but I really need legal advice."`** → `Reassure them — the Department offers free Voluntary Legal Services (Pro Bono). Help them register on the Voluntary Legal Services Smart Portal (also Smart App / DubaiNow), choose the area of law, and they'll be matched with a volunteer lawyer. No fee.`
4. **`"How do I know if 'XYZ Law Firm' is actually licensed?"`** → `Great question to encourage. Use the Advocates & Legal Consultants Directory on the website — search by the firm or person's name (full or partial) to confirm they're licensed. Offer to show them how.`
5. **`"Our government department needs a contract reviewed — urgently."`** → `That's Government Legal Support via the Central Legal Services Portal (Gov Portal) — contract drafting, review and advice. Typically 10–30 working days by request type; no fee. Help them find the portal and the documents to attach.`
6. **`"I want to file a criminal case against my neighbour!"`** → `Empathise, then redirect correctly. The Legal Affairs Department does NOT handle criminal reports or complaints — politely direct them to Dubai Police / Public Prosecution. (For a non‑criminal dispute with a government entity, the Department can help under Law No. 16 of 2025.)`
7. **`(A visitor using a wheelchair approaches your desk.)`** → `Lead with the Charter: serve People of Determination in line with their needs. Proactively offer help, ensure the channel is accessible, be patient, and guide them through the service or smart channel yourself.`
